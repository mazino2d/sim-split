SimSplit makes it easy to track and split shared expenses with friends, family, or colleagues — completely offline. No account or internet connection required.

FEATURES:
• Create groups for trips, households, or any shared expense
• Add expenses and split them equally, by percentage, by exact amount, or by shares
• See who owes whom at a glance with automatic debt simplification
• Record settlements when debts are paid
• Export expense summaries to share with the group
• Supports VND, USD, EUR, and more
• Available in Vietnamese and English

WORKS OFFLINE:
All data is stored securely on your device. SimSplit works without internet — perfect for trips or places with poor connectivity.

NO ACCOUNT NEEDED:
Just open the app and start tracking. No sign-up, no email, no password.
