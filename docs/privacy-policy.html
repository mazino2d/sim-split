Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Short version: SimSplit works entirely offline. We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

SimSplit does not collect any personal information. The app has no internet permission and cannot send data anywhere.

The following information is stored locally on your device only:

  • Group names and settings you create
  • Member names you enter within groups
  • Expense records (title, amount, payer, split details)
  • Settlement records between members

This data never leaves your device and is inaccessible to us or any third party.

2. How We Use Your Information

All data is used solely to provide the app's core functionality — splitting expenses and tracking settlements — within the app on your device. We have no access to this data.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share, sell, rent, or transfer your data to any third party. SimSplit contains no:

  • Analytics or tracking SDKs
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • Cloud sync or backup services

4. Data Storage and Security

All app data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device using Android's standard app storage (not accessible to other apps). Deleting the app removes all associated data permanently.

5. Device Permissions

SimSplit requests no sensitive device permissions. The app does not access your camera, microphone, location, contacts, or any other device resources beyond local app storage.

6. Children's Privacy

SimSplit does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected or transmitted, the app presents no privacy risk to users of any age.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be published at this URL with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
